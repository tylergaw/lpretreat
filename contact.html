Hours of Operation

We are available for services Tuesday thru Saturday by appointment only. Please call ahead of time to make sure someone is here to assist you.

Directions

From Highway 61, take the Highway UU exit. We are the third drive on the left. Look for our sign by the road. The salon/spa is located in the lower level of the gray ranch. Another Spa sign will direct you to make a left turn to the parking area and entrance.
