Spa Tips

To make everyone's day at the spa more relaxing...

Appointments

Please be on time for your scheduled appointment to receive full benefit of your spa treatment. Your appointment will end on time out of respect for the appointments scheduled after you.

Spa Voices

While visiting the spa please lower your voice in consideration of those receiving relaxing treatments.

Cellular Phones

In order to help preserve our serene atmosphere, please turn your cell phones off or to silent mode so as not to distract clients that are here to relax.

Pedicures

Don't forget your thongs so you can be sure to maintain your beautiful pedicure until it is completely dry.

Waxing

For the best results, allow at least 7 to 10 days growth before making an appointment for waxing services.

Deposits

Must be paid with a credit card for the full amount. You will then be issued a gift card to be used upon the day of your services. Any cancellations must be made 48 hours in advance. If not, gift card will be reduced by 50% of its original value.

Payment for Services

We accept cash, check, Mastercard and Visa.

Children

We love them as well, but a spa is not the place for children under high school age, it is our goal to keep our salon a relaxing environment for all of our clients.
