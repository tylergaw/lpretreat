Pampering Pedicures

Express Pedicure

30 min. - $20.00
For those on the go or in need of a quick fix. Trim nails and cuticles, buff and shine or add polish.

La Petite Spa Pedicure

1 Hour - $30.00
Our signature pedicure. Includes foot soak, nail and cuticle care, callous removal and foot and leg massage. Finish with buff and shine or add polish.

Raw Earth Pedicure

75 min. - $50.00
Earth derived pedicure filled with natural ingrdients rich in vitamins, minerals, antioxidants and essential oils to moisturize, smooth and polish the skin. Begins with a fizzing mineral soak, exfoliation with a warming raw sugar scrub, next we apply a conditioning volcanic clay foot masque. Nails and cuticles are trimmed before receiving a vitamin and mineral moisture foot and leg massage. Finish with buff and shine or add polish.

Ritas on the Rocks

90 min. - $65.00
A refreshing pick me up that leaves skin smooth and glistening. Starts with a fragrant citrus soak and fresh lime slices on top and smooth river rocks on the bottom. A vigorous lime and salt scrub wakes up the foot and legs. Nails and cuticles are trimmed before a luxurious massage with fresh crushed avacado mixed with green tea lotion. Skin receives extra pampering with grapefruit oil moisturizer to help reduce age spots. Finish with buff and shine or add polish.
