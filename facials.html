Facial Rejuvenation

All facial treatments are performed with MD Formulations.

Your facial rejuvenation begins with a skin analysis by your licensed Esthetician to customize each treatment for your particular needs. Included in your treatment is a relaxing hand, arm, neck and shoulder massage.

European Spa Facial

1 Hour - $55.00
Corrective w/extractions - $65.00
Deep cleansing and exfoliation of the face.
Eye treatment may be added to help eliminate puffiness and dark circles - $15.00

Gentlemen's Facial

1 Hour - $60.00
Focuses on men's specific skin conditions. Includes deep cleansing, exfoliation, steaming and treatment masque that calms skin irritated by daily shaving. Relaxing scalp, neck, shoulders, arm and hand massage complete this treatment.

Teen Facial

45 minutes - $35.00
An introduction to skin care. Appropriate skin care can minimize blemishes, oil and problem breakouts. Facial focuses on how to care for skin at home.

Express Alpha/Beta Peel

$50.00 per peel or $250.00 for six-week series
This quick "lunchtime" peel works to immediately reveal softer, smoother, more radiant skin without the visible peeling, redness and downtime associated with deep chemical peels. Instead a gradual approach is taken. While one peel will deliver visible skin renewal, a series of peels is recommended for the most dramatic results. Ideal for all skin types and conditions except extremely sensitive skin.
